How It Works

A Seamless Experience From Start to Finish

We have refined our process over 1,500+ projects to ensure every step is smooth, transparent, and stress-free. Here is exactly what to expect.

01

Initial Consultation

Contact us by phone, email, or our online form. We discuss your project, answer initial questions, and schedule an in-home visit if needed. Most consultations happen within 24 to 48 hours of your first call.

Free • No Obligation
02

Material Selection

Browse our extensive inventory or visit our partner showrooms. We guide you through granite, quartz, quartzite, marble, and exotic options — helping you match material to your lifestyle, design, and budget.

Expert Guidance Included
03

Detailed Estimate

We prepare a comprehensive, transparent estimate covering material costs, fabrication, edge profiles, cutouts, and installation. No hidden fees, no surprises. You know exactly what you are paying for.

Transparent Pricing
04

Client Decision

Take the time you need. Once you approve the estimate and confirm your material and edge selections, we lock in your project and begin scheduling the next phases.

No Pressure
05

Material Order

We order your selected slabs from our trusted providers. For in-stock materials, this is immediate. For special orders, we provide clear delivery timelines and keep you updated.

Direct from Top Suppliers
06

Material Delivery & Inspection

Your stone arrives at our Steamboat Springs workshop. Every slab undergoes a quality inspection to verify color, pattern, and structural integrity before fabrication begins.

Quality Verified
07

Digital Templating

Our team visits your home with precision digital measuring tools to create an exact template of your space. Every angle, curve, and cutout is mapped to the millimeter.

Laser Precision
08

Fabrication

Your countertops are precision-cut using CNC technology, then hand-finished by our master craftsmen. Edges are profiled, surfaces are polished, and every detail is inspected before leaving the workshop.

In-House • Steamboat Springs
09

Professional Installation

Our dedicated installation crew arrives with your finished countertops. Most installations are completed in a single day. We handle leveling, adhesion, seam work, sink connections, and final sealing.

Usually Same Day
10

Final Walkthrough & Care Guide

We walk you through the finished project, teach you proper care and maintenance, and ensure every detail meets your expectations. Your satisfaction is the only finish line that matters.

100% Satisfaction
