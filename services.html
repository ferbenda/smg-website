Residential Services

Kitchen Countertops

The heart of your home deserves a surface that is both stunning and built to last. We fabricate and install kitchen countertops in granite, quartz, quartzite, marble, and other premium materials.

Our process includes precise digital templating, custom edge profiling, undermount sink cutouts, cooktop openings, and seamless installation — typically completed in a single day.

Every kitchen project includes a complimentary design consultation where we guide you on material selection, layout optimization, seam placement, and edge profiles.

Bathroom Vanities

Transform your bathroom into a spa-like retreat. We specialize in single and double vanity countertops with custom sink cutouts, vessel sink installations, and edge treatments.

Quartz and quartzite are popular bathroom choices for their moisture resistance and low maintenance.

We handle everything from master bath suites to powder rooms and guest bathrooms. Each project is precision-fitted to your cabinetry.

Master BathGuest BathPowder RoomVessel Sink

Full-Height Backsplashes

Eliminate grout lines and create a seamless, dramatic wall surface. Full-height stone backsplashes extend from your countertop to the upper cabinets or ceiling for maximum visual impact.

Easier to clean than tile, grease and moisture-proof, and visually stunning. We match backsplash veining to your countertop for a continuous, cohesive look.

Fireplaces & Hearths

Make your fireplace the focal point of any room. We fabricate custom stone surrounds, mantels, and hearths that transform a standard fireplace into an architectural statement.

Heat-resistant granite and quartzite are ideal choices. We can create floor-to-ceiling installations with bookmatched slabs for maximum dramatic effect.

SurroundsMantelsHearthsFloor to Ceiling

Waterfall Islands

The ultimate statement piece. Waterfall edges cascade stone from the countertop surface down to the floor on one or both sides of your island, creating a striking continuous flow.

Requires precision mitering and vein matching at the corners. Our master fabricators specialize in making the seam virtually invisible for a truly seamless waterfall effect.
