Where Mountain Living Meets Master Craftsmanship

Steamboat Masters Granite was founded in 2019 in the heart of Steamboat Springs, Colorado. What began as a commitment to raising the standard of countertop fabrication in the Yampa Valley has grown into one of the region's most trusted names in premium stone surfaces.

Our team brings over 25 years of collective expertise to every project. We are not just installers — we are artisans who study the natural veining of every slab, plan every cut for maximum beauty, and treat every home as if it were our own.

We believe the best results come from a consultant-first approach. Before we cut a single slab, we guide you through material selection, layout planning, edge profiles, and long-term durability — ensuring your investment looks stunning for decades.

More Than Fabricators — Your Consultants

At Steamboat Masters Granite, we take a consultant approach that sets us apart from big-box stores and volume shops. We stay responsive and own every project like it is our own home.

Our team is highly trained in CNC technology and hand-finishing techniques, combining the precision of modern fabrication with the irreplaceable touch of human craftsmanship.

We work with an extensive network of stone suppliers nationwide, meaning we can source virtually any material you have in mind — from local favorites to rare exotic stones from quarries around the world.
