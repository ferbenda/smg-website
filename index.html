What We Do

Your Countertop Experts — From First Call to Final Polish

More than fabricators — we are full-service countertop specialists serving Steamboat Springs, Routt County, and Northwest Colorado. From material consulting to precision installation, we own every step of the process.

01

Consultation & Estimation

We start where every great project begins — with your vision. Our countertop specialists assess your space, understand your lifestyle, and build a detailed estimate from the ground up. We guide you through material options, edge profiles, sink configurations, and layout decisions with full transparency at every stage.

Already have a material selected or a preferred supplier? No problem. We work alongside your choices and manage the provider relationship end-to-end — or step back and let you lead. Either way, you always know exactly what you're getting and what it costs.

02

Precision Fabrication

Every slab that enters our Steamboat Springs fabrication shop is treated as a one-of-a-kind investment. Our cutting process is engineered to maximize material yield — minimizing waste while preserving the natural flow and veining of each stone.

From CNC-precision cutting and sink cutouts to edge profiling and final polish, every detail is executed in a single integrated workflow. Granite, quartz, marble, quartzite, soapstone, porcelain — each material gets the exact technique it demands. The result: surfaces that fit perfectly, look flawless, and last a lifetime.

03

Professional Installation

When you give us the green light, we move. Our installation crews are highly responsive, organized, and laser-focused on completing your project as quickly and seamlessly as possible — without cutting corners.

Whether your project is in Steamboat Springs, Routt County, or anywhere within 150 miles, you get the same process, the same standards, and the same timeline. Distance never affects the quality or efficiency of our work.

We keep you informed at every step: arrival times, progress updates, and post-installation walkthroughs are all part of how we operate. We treat your home with the same care we'd give our own — and we don't leave until everything is perfect.

04

Post-Installation Support

Our commitment doesn't end at installation. Any questions, adjustments, or follow-up requests after your project is complete are handled with the same professionalism and urgency as day one.

We are always reachable, always accountable, and always focused on making sure you are fully satisfied — long after our crew has left. At Steamboat Masters Granite, post-sale support isn't an afterthought. It's part of what makes us Northwest Colorado's most trusted countertop specialists.

05

Remnants & Premium Materials

Looking for exceptional stone at a better price point? Our in-house inventory of granite, quartz, and natural stone remnants offers the same quality craftsmanship at a fraction of the cost — perfect for smaller projects like bathroom vanities, laundry rooms, bar tops, and outdoor surfaces.

We also carry premium slabs acquired through exclusive supplier bundles and sales from our network of industry-leading providers. Whether you're working with a tight budget or simply want the best value on high-end stone, our inventory gives Steamboat Springs and Northwest Colorado homeowners direct access to materials that are rarely available elsewhere.

Ask us about current remnant availability — stock changes frequently.

How It Works

A Seamless Experience

From your first message to your final reveal — we make every step simple, transparent, and built around you.

01

Consultation & Estimation

Reach out however works best for you — phone, text, email, social media, or online form. We adapt to you. Once we connect, we'll gather the details we need: measurements, photos, project scope, and your vision. No site visit required unless you prefer one.

We are highly responsive across every channel and committed to delivering your detailed estimate within 24 to 48 hours — so you can move forward with confidence and without waiting.

02

Material Selection

Not sure what you want? We'll guide you. Already have something in mind? We'll make it happen. Our team works directly with our premium supplier network to confirm availability, pricing, and lead times in real time — so material decisions are fast, informed, and stress-free.

Once your material is selected and secured, delivery to our Steamboat Springs facility takes 2 to 3 weeks — and fabrication begins immediately upon arrival.

03

Templating & Fabrication

Before a single cut is made, our team visits your space to capture a precise, fully customized template of your countertop layout. Every angle, cutout, and edge detail is measured to exact specifications — this is where perfection starts.

From there, fabrication takes place in our Steamboat Springs shop and is typically completed within one week. Your project is prioritized from start to finish — no delays, no shortcuts. CNC precision cutting, sink cutouts, edge profiling, and final polish are all executed in a single integrated workflow built for quality and efficiency.

04

Installation

Once your countertops are ready, we confirm with you directly and wait for your green light. Our crews are organized, punctual, and focused on one thing: completing your installation as cleanly and efficiently as possible.

Most projects are completed in 2 to 3 days depending on scope and complexity. From the moment we arrive to the final walkthrough, we maintain clear communication, protect your space, and ensure every surface is seated, sealed, and finished to perfection before we leave.

05

Post-Sale Support & Lifetime Guarantee

Our relationship with you doesn't end at installation — it's just getting started. Whether you have a maintenance question, need guidance on care products, or simply want to check in, we are always available and always happy to help.

We are so confident in the quality of our work that we back every project with a lifetime guarantee on fabrication and installation. If something isn't right, we make it right — no questions asked.

At Steamboat Masters Granite, the job isn't done until you're completely satisfied.
